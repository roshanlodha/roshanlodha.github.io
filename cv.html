Non-random Rotation Matching Algorithm (NRMA)

Craig Nielsen, M.D., 2023

  • Developed NRMA, a non-random rotation matching algorithm for medical students, utilizing linear sum optimization techniques to match students with their rotation preferences.
  • Implemented and tested the algorithm to ensure efficient and accurate matching, resulting in improved student satisfaction and reduced administrative burden.
  • Collaborated with medical faculty and staff to gather rotation preference data, incorporate feedback, and continuously improve the algorithm over multiple iterations.

Biochemical Oncology - Feng Lab, University of California, San Fransisco
