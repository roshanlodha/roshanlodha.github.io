Privacy Policy for Uworld2Anki

Last Updated: Sunday March 23, 2025

Thank you for using Uworld2Anki ("the Extension"). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any information that the extension may access or store and ensures that your data remains secure.

Information Collection and Storage

The Uworld2Anki extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. All configuration settings (such as your OpenAI API key, prompt template, model, max tokens, and temperature) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's chrome.storage.sync API. This data is used solely to customize your experience and is not transmitted to any external servers.

How Your Information is Used

All settings you enter into Uworld2Anki remain on your device. The extension operates entirely on your local machine, and we do not have access to any of your data. Any interactions with the OpenAI API are performed based on your inputs and settings, and we do not log or record these interactions.

Third-Party Services

The extension makes use of the OpenAI API to generate content. For information on how OpenAI handles data, please refer to their Privacy Policy. We do not control OpenAI's data practices, so we encourage you to review their policies.
