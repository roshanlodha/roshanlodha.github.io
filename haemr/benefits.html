💡Why the HDHP can win here: Preventive care is $0 on all three plans. If little non-preventive care is expected, the HDHP deductible may never meaningfully come into play, while the $500 HSA seed creates immediate tax-advantaged value that can be saved or invested for future health expenses.
⚠️Main tradeoff: An unexpected medical event on the HDHP can require up to $2,000 out of pocket before the richer coverage begins, versus materially lower first-dollar exposure on the EPO. Anyone who prefers predictability over upside from the HSA seed may reasonably default to the EPO.

What Happens to Each Policy When You Leave MGB

Today → Residency End (~2029)
Both coverages active
Group LTD is free and covers 60% of the PGY salary (~$4,229/mo gross, taxable). An individual policy can layer on top of that base coverage during training.
Day You Leave MGB
Group LTD terminates immediately
The moment MGB employment ends, the group LTD policy ends. A conversion option typically exists but is expensive with inferior terms. Pre-existing conditions developed during residency may be excluded from a new employer's group plan.
Attending Year 1 — Same Day
Guardian IDI keeps paying — at EM attending rates
If an individual policy was purchased during residency, it remains active through job changes. The FIO rider allows benefit increases as income rises, without new medical underwriting, at rates tied to the original issue date.
Any Point During Career
True Own-Occupation for EM matters enormously
If an injury prevents hands-on EM care, a true own-occupation policy can continue paying even if the physician later works in teaching, consulting, or administration. That distinction is especially meaningful for physically demanding, procedural specialties.

🚨Critical sequencing rule: Guardian's GSI offer at MGB requires applying with Guardian first — before any other disability insurance company. Applying elsewhere first (Standard, Ameritas, Principal) forfeits eligibility for the no-underwriting GSI program.
📞Quote comparison: Review the resident-specific Guardian pathway at residentgme.com and compare it with other resident-discount offerings before deciding. The main objective is to compare portability, definition of disability, riders, and long-term premium structure on the same benefit amount.
💰The cost of waiting: A policy purchased during training can lock in resident discounts and avoid future underwriting surprises. The same coverage bought after residency often costs materially more and may come with exclusions that were not present earlier in training.

Prioritized Action Items

  • 1
    Complete onboarding paperwork before the stated program deadline Benefits elections and payroll setup usually depend on the core employment onboarding items being finished first.
  • 2
    Elect HDHP + Core Dental + Vision during initial enrollment if using the lean setup No waiting period is assumed here. The employer HSA seed typically posts early in the academic year after HDHP enrollment.
  • 3
    If evaluating Guardian's GSI pathway, review it before submitting other disability applications The sequencing matters because applying elsewhere first can forfeit guaranteed-issue access through the resident program.
  • 4
    Decide whether to keep the HSA in cash or invest it once the balance threshold is met Unused HSA dollars roll forward indefinitely and can be reserved for future medical, dental, or vision spending.
  • 5
    Contribute at least 2% to the 403(b) from Day 1 MGB matches 100% up to 2% of pay, so this is one of the clearest immediate return opportunities in the package.
  • 6
    Enroll in MBTA subsidy immediately The modeled out-of-pocket cost is roughly ~$45/month instead of a full-price pass, using pre-tax payroll deduction.
  • 7
    Register for One Medical (free) If the membership is included, registration is simple and can provide convenient access to same-day primary care when needed.
  • 8
    Confirm exact dental and vision payroll deductions with HR before finalizing the budget The benefit guides summarize plan design clearly, but exact paycheck deductions should still be verified directly through the employer enrollment materials.
